Acronym Passphrase Generator How it Works

Welcome to the Acronym Passphrase Generator.

This generator gives you an Acronym Passphrase Generator. It works similarly to the Dice Ware Passphrase generator.
But instead of generating a purely random passphrase it generates a Acronym passphrase.
You can enter a meaningful word to you, and a Passphrase will be generated with random words
that start with each of the characters that make up the word you entered.
This means you lose randomness, therefore knowing the initials of every word of the passphrase makes it easier to remember.

Steps:

1. Input a .txt file with the Wordlist you want to use. Remember it must follow the DiceWare Algorithm.

2. Enter how many dice throws your Wordlist requires. The default Wordlists provided use 5 Dice throws.

3. Enter the meaningful word to form the Acronym Passphrase. Remember the passphrase's length will match the number of letters in the word you enter.

4. Click on the Generate Button and see the results.
